Q: What is ezcarrot?

A: ezcarrot is a mobile app that allows you to upload a screenshot of your social media post, online review, text message, email, etc. that promotes a business. The business can then reward you directly for promoting their business.

Q: How do I find a business that I promoted or want to promote?

A: Use the search bar on the mobile app to find a business that you promoted or to find a business that you would like to promote. ezcarrot allows you to see what on average businesses are paying for screenshots to give you an idea of how much your screenshots might be worth.

Q: How do I upload a screenshot? How do I upload a screenshot?

A: After you promote a business through social media, online reviews, text messages, emails, etc. take a screenshot of what you did with your mobile device. On the app, search for the business that you promoted and then click the upload button to upload the screenshot from your camera roll. Another option is to upload a screenshot from your camera roll first and then select a business.

Q: How do I get paid?

A: After you upload a screenshot to a business, ezcarrot's automated software will immediately analyze the screenshot and give a recommendation to the business on the value of the screenshot. The business will then make a final decision on whether to reward the screenshot and if so for how much. If the business decides to pay you for the screenshot this will be reflected in your account balance. To redeem, you must have an account balance of at least $20.00. Click the redeem button to have a check mailed to your address for the value of your account balance.

Q: If I upload a screenshot will I get paid

A: It is up to the business to decide whether or not you will get paid. There is no guarantee that you will get paid for your screenshot.

Q: Are all rewards cash rewards?

A: All rewards paid by businesses are cash rewards.

Q: Is there a check writing fee?

A: Every time you redeem your account balance there is a $0.99 check writing fee.
